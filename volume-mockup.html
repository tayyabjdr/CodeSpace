Recommendation

Option A — the speaker-icon-with-popover hits the right balance for this aesthetic. It keeps the toolbar clean (no slider eating horizontal space when you're not adjusting), gives you a real volume slider plus a mute toggle in one surface, and the icon button matches the visual rhythm of the existing window controls. Option B is great for power users who want zero-click adjustment but pollutes the titlebar with a colorful slider that's always demanding attention. Option C is the cleanest but you can't actually adjust how loud the ding is — only on/off.
